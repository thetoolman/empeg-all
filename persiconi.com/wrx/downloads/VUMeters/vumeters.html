Analogue VU Meters:

The 2.0 version of the empeg software (which is still in beta) has a cool new visual called "Anna VU".  This mimics an analogue VU meter.  Some smart people at the empeg BBS found that you can edit and create your own back grounds for the moving needles.

After being unzipped, the files below need to be saved as "vuback.raw" (all lowercase) on the car player.  Complete detailed instructions can be found here courtesy of Tony Fabris and the rest of the empeg community.  Most of the zipped files linked below include previews in .gif and/or .png formats, the vuback.raw file and a "how-to" in .txt format.

Note: The color of the meter is dependant on the lens color of your player.  I provided examples in all the colors so you can get an idea what it looks like.  Also, iIf you mess up your player by hacking into it, it is your mess to fix not mine.
