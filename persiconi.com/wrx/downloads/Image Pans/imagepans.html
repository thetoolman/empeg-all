Image Pans:

After being unzipped, the files below need to be saved as "escher.raw" (all lowercase) on the car player.  Complete detailed instructions can be found here courtesy of Tony Fabris and the rest of the empeg community.  Even though this is primarily a resource for fellow Subaru owners, I do take requests.  Most of the zipped files linked below include previews in .gif and/or .png formats, the escher.raw file and a "how-to" in .txt format.

Note:  Most of these images, are, of course, just rips of pictures found on the web and the sources of those images are copyrighted in and of themselves.  In fact, most of that previous sentence was taken from someone else's quote.  I offer these files for your enjoyment and with the full understanding that they come with no warranty or promises.  If you mess up your player by hacking into it, it is your mess to fix not mine.
