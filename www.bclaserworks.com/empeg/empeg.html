Empeg MK2 and RioCar Reproduction Lenses

Lenses are lasercut from 0.06"acrylic to replicate the original Empeg MK2/ RioCar lenses. Available in 5 colors (Smoke, Dark Smoke, Bronze, Green, and Red).

Please note: Every effort has been made to match the colors of the Empeg photos to reality but they are not exact. I have retouched a Smoke Lens image to "simulate" the look of the Bronze and Dark Smoke Lenses.
