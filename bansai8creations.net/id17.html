Designed specifically for the empeg Mark1, Mark2, and Mark2A(RIO) car stereo!

This leather case utilizes the same foam used for coast guard life vests to protect the stereo from damage if dropped, and it might even keep the embag afloat in the unlikely event of a water landing!


Black leather with black side snap closure.

Mahogany leather with antique brass flap clasp.

Standard design incorporates a comfortable handle made using the same leather as the bag body.

The design incorporates material within the flap to distribute the weight to minimize any "sags" (think: three point bending) that may occur with having only one closure.

There is an option for a shoulder strap with additional mounting D-rings on the sides (currently only a black strap is available).


Mahogany leather and olive suede covered foam padding.

The foam is notched at the top so the face plate fits in snugly & safely within the bag.

This is designed to work specifically with the Mark1, Mark2, and Mark2A (RIO) car stereos.

But the case should also accommodate any other 1-DIN car stereo.


Black leather "standard" has heavy duty black coil zipper.

Mahogany leather with brass zipper and flap clasp.
A large zippered pocket on the front provides additional capacity for carrying your cell phone, camera, small tools, etc.

The standard design uses a heavy-duty black plastic coil zipper--as used in commercial bags & packs.

Zippers with brass or nickel teeth are "custom" options.

The standard colors currently available for embag are L to R: black, mahogany, and tan. Swatches are available!

Please contact me about other colors. I will attempt to accomodate any request for your custom order!
